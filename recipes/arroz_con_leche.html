Arroz con Leche

Description:

Tasty dessert made up of rice and milk.

Ingredients:

  • Milk or Almond milk (for dairy free)
  • Some ground turmeric
  • Some ground ginger or chopped fresh ginger
  • ground black pepper
  • sweetener of choice: honey, sugar, maple syrup...
  • Rice
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract
  • A lime
